About Us

We are the Modern Western Elevators Company for elevators and escalators, agents of (MODERn FUJI). Our work team consists of engineers and technicians who have the experience and high precision required to perform tasks and designs in the best ways and with modern technology.

  • All elevator missions from major international and local companies.

  • Our company is committed to all technical terms and specifications for elevators.

  • Our company is committed to the Saudi code to update the design principles and conditions for implementing electric elevators in buildings.

  • Our bid includes a full explanation of the elevator components and their technical specifications.

  • The company has ready stock for standard elevators.

  • Our company submits the executive drawings immediately upon contracting, which include all the technical details of the elevators subject to the contract and conform to the Saudi standard specifications (dimensions of the shaft and the machine room, the free distance below the shaft, the engine capacity, the elevator load, the dimensions of the elevator, the number of stops, the dimensions of the threshold doors, the position of the counterweight, and also showing the sections of the guides for the movement of the elevator, the counterweight, and the loads in the machine room). They are reviewed and approved by the owner or his supervisory authority, and they are among the documents complementing the contract.

  • Our company is fully prepared to implement any urgent schedule proposed by the owner so that we can organize the tasks and start implementation immediately upon contracting, except for the cadres and risers, which are implemented according to what the final dimensions of each well allow separately and are linked to the owner’s approval of the executive drawings submitted by our company.
